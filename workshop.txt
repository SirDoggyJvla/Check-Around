description=Original concept by Braven with his [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3044807092]Stairs Alert[/url] mod.
description=
description=This is a complete new take on the mechanic, with fresh code and proper check. The original mod by Braven didn't properly detect zombies so this version fixes this and improves everything.
description=
description=[quote]
description=[h1]How to use:[/h1]
description=[list]
description=[*] When in stairs, press the keybind to check upstairs. By default, it is the same key as "Interact": E.
description=[*] This will check in a circle around the square at the top of the stairs.
description=[*] If any zombies are detected, their amount will be shown above the player.
description=[*] If no zombies are detected, your player will mention it.
description=[/list]
description=[/quote]
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Customize your experience:[/h1]
description=[list]
description=[*] Change your [b]keybind[/b] thanks to [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2169435993]Mod Options[/url] !
description=[*] Adapt your sandbox options to your liking ! You can increase the radius which the character checks for zombies at the top of the stairs. You can chose to show the zombie position or not.
description=[*] Chose the lore name of the your zombies ! This can also be done with a mod (check the next part about modder's documentation).
description=[*] Native compatibility with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=3243131044]Zomboid Forge[/url] ! This will show the name of the zombie type instead simply "zombie" and take into consideration the nametag options of the user (font, duration...).
description=[/list]
description=[/quote]
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Customization available to other modders[/h1]
description=Other modders can easily modify the mod and its parameters ! Add your own voicelines or modify the name to show above zombies or in the voicelines ! For example, [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3248766883]The Last of Us Infected[/url] will not mention "zombies" but instead "infected".
